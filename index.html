How to Make Filled Aubergines

Filled & Baked Eggplant is a very tasteful dish. On top of that, all the different vegetables and minced meat make your stomach full and satisfied.

Order

Ingredients

  • 7 Eggplants
  • 3 Onions
  • 1 kg Minced Meat
  • 4 Peppers
  • 1 glass of Rice
  • Salt & Pepper & Chili Powder

Preparation

  1. Chop the onions, fry with minced meat.
  2. Adjust peppers and tomato paste, do not forget to stir!
  3. Add the rice and 1 glass of hot water to finish the sauce
  4. Fill in the oven at 170 degrees. Give it 30 minutes for baking.
  5. Enjoy your meal!
